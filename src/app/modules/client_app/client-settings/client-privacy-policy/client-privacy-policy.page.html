iFS360 Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Focus Security Services Pte Ltd ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) of Singapore.

This Policy applies to all users of the iFS360 software application, including:

  • Residents
  • Employees of clients
  • Client administrators and management

By using iFS360, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal data as described in this Policy.

2. Definitions

  • Personal Data: Data that can identify an individual, whether directly or indirectly.
  • Application: Refers to iFS360 software.
  • User: Any individual accessing or using the Application.
  • PDPA: Personal Data Protection Act 2012 of Singapore.

3. Personal Data We Collect

3.1. Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Residential or workplace address
  • Identification details (where required for access control)

3.2. Usage Data

  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • Login timestamps and activity logs
  • Pages accessed within the app

3.3. Application Permissions (With Consent)

  • Location data (for access verification / site-based services)
  • Camera/photos (for incident reporting, visitor records, or verification)
  • Contact list (if required for operational features)

4. Purpose of Collection, Use and Disclosure

We collect and use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the iFS360 system
  • To manage user accounts and authentication
  • To facilitate security operations (e.g. visitor management, incident reporting)
  • To communicate with users (alerts, updates, security notifications)
  • To improve system functionality and user experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • To investigate incidents, complaints, or breaches

We will not use your personal data for purposes other than those stated unless we obtain your consent or are required by law.

5. Consent

By using iFS360 or providing your personal data, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your data for the purposes stated.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us (see Section 14). Please note that withdrawal of consent may affect your ability to use the features of the Application.

6. Disclosure of Personal Data

We may disclose your personal data to:

  • Service providers (e.g. hosting, IT support)
  • Clients and property management (for operational purposes)
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required
  • Business partners where necessary for service delivery

All third parties are required to protect your data in accordance with PDPA.

7. Data Storage (Amazon Web Services)

Your data is securely stored on Amazon Web Services (AWS) servers. We ensure that:

  • Data is stored in secure cloud infrastructure
  • Access is restricted and controlled
  • Industry-standard encryption and security measures are applied

8. Transfer of Personal Data Outside Singapore

Where your data is transferred outside Singapore (e.g. AWS servers), we ensure that:

  • The receiving party provides a standard of protection comparable to PDPA
  • Appropriate safeguards and contractual protections are in place

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfil the purposes stated in this Policy
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Data no longer required will be securely deleted or anonymised.

10. Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data

Requests can be made by contacting us (Section 14). We may charge a reasonable administrative fee where permitted under PDPA.

11. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Access control and authentication systems
  • Regular system monitoring and audits
  • Secure hosting environment (AWS)

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission is completely secure.

12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach:

  • We will assess the severity of the breach
  • Notify the Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) where required
  • Inform affected individuals if the breach is likely to result in significant harm

13. Do Not Call (DNC) Provisions

Where applicable, we comply with Singapore's Do Not Call (DNC) Registry regulations. We will not send marketing messages via calls, SMS, or other means unless you have provided consent, or it is permitted under applicable law.
